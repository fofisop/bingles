What are some of the best things to do when visiting the capital of Ohio?

Let's find out!

Scroll along the website and discover new places for your next trip here!

Franklin park conservatory and botanical gardens

With seasonal exhibits and lots of beautiful plants, there is always lots to see here!

It's like a dream park!

German Village

A small German village with shops and a spot to eat!

I highly reccomend you try their cream puffs, they are the best.

Columbus Zoo

A nice sized zoo with lots of epic exibits to see!

Very well organized as well.

Mardi Gras

The best ice cream I have ever had.

If you are bored of vanilla and chocolate, try Mardi Gras ice cream! They have Indian flavors such as jasmine, ginger, and mango.

Cosi

An awesome family friendly science center built for learning and fun!

It even features some historic replicas of towns that you can explore and get great photo opportunities!
